Garlic

What is Garlic?

Garlic is a pungent herb more like a vegetable we eat everyday that smells like horrid breath.

Why Grow Garlic?

Unlike you, garlic has skill, talent and goals, & worth more than a tenfold more than your useless body will ever be. If you want to be successful like garlic, GROW THIS NOW!!!!!

What do you need to grow Garlic?

The Ingredients:

  • High Quality soil or dirt
  • single garlic clove
  • outside or small pot to hold soil
  • Human Hands,eyes & fingers
  • common sense & a brain
  • water

Instructions - How to Grow the Garlic

  1. Start by using common sense and put soil in a small pot.
    • Soil 4/5th to the top
  2. After, poke a hole in middle of soil with pinky finger.
  3. Third, insert garlic clove in the hole.
    • Garlic clove pointy side has to face the ground/bottom.
  4. Cover the garlic clove with more soil.
  5. Finally, every day, spray water on the soil lightly every day once in evening. Sisyphean task.
  6. Harvest/take out the plant lightly from the soil after the green sprout bottom leaves have died and are continuing.
